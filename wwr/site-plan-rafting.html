Site Plan

Overview

Purpose

The website will convey the fun we can have on the river, sell the supplies required, and promote river running as a whole. The entire family needs to be advertised as having fun and that it is a safe activity for all no matter if they are fit or handicapped. One day and two day trips will be the most advertised as well as week long trips for the more adventurous individuals. The main message of the site: “Rafting is a fun activity for all to enjoy. Come and experience it for yourself and bring your family.

Audience

Target audience:
Adventurous
Mid 20's to 50's
Upper middle class to upper class income
Healthy lifestyle
Family oriented for one and two day trips
Single professionals for week long trips.

What do they want?
These people want a fun adventure to share with family or friends/co-workers.
They want to experience something new to them.
They want to re-experience the adrenaline rush you get while rafting.

Needs to being met?
Outdoor supplies relating to water activities
Fun and safe trips to satisfy an adrenalin rush

What devices will be used to access the site?
All available.
