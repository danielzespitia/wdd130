White Water Rafthing

The trip to the waterfalls was an extraordinary experience. From the moment we arrived at the destination, we were fascinated by the natural beauty that surrounded us. The waterfalls were impressive, with their crystal clear water falling majestically and creating a unique visual and sound spectacle.
The travel product exceeded all of our expectations. The itinerary was perfectly organized, allowing us to enjoy each waterfall in detail. The tour guides were extremely knowledgeable and friendly, providing us with interesting information about the flora, fauna, and history of each place we visited.

History

Once upon a time there was a company called Dry Oar Cascadas Travel, specialized in organizing exciting adventures in some of the most beautiful destinations in the world. Founded by two friends passionate about nature, Laura and Carlos, Dry Oar quickly became a benchmark in the adventure tourism industry.
The story of Dry Oar began when Laura and Carlos, lovers of water sports and exploration, decided to share their passion with the world. After years of planning and preparation, they launched their travel agency with a unique focus: taking travelers to discover the wonders of waterfalls.
